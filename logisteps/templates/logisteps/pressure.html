What's this?

This graphic visualizes the average pressure placed on each sensor for the past day, week, and month. This data may be used to help you identify unhealthy amounts of pressure being applied to parts of the foot and make adjustments. By displaying average pressure over the past day, week, and month, you can track their progress. As more sensors are added to the smartsole technology, this visual will relect a visual that is closer and closer to a pressure gradient map.
